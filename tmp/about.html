Who We Are

Tom Mboya University Black Market is a student-run store that provides affordable and high-quality academic supplies, textbooks, and campus essentials to the university community. Founded in 2020 by a group of entrepreneurial students, our store has grown to become the go-to destination for all campus needs.

Our mission is to make student life easier by offering essential products at student-friendly prices, helping everyone succeed academically without breaking the bank.
